# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- demonstrating empathy and respect;
- giving and accepting constructive feedback;
- focusing on what is best for the project and community;
- taking responsibility, apologizing, and learning from mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized attention, insults,
threats, publication of private information, and other conduct reasonably
considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Report
incidents privately through the security contact described in
`docs/security.md`.

Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of reporters.
